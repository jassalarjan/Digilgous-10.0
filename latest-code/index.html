About us

It is our firm conviction that technology possesses the power to revolutionize the world and enhance the quality of people's lives. Throughout the past few decades, it has brought about a profound transformation in the way individuals live, work, engage in leisure activities, and establish connections. The remarkable achievements of technology visionaries have played a pivotal role in the overall advancement of our nation. In order to commemorate the invaluable contributions made by these great technologists and to inspire the widespread utilization of information and communication technology in diverse fields such as Science, Agriculture, Defence, Entertainment, and more,we proudly present 'K. C Garg Memorial Inter School Tech Symposium : Digilogous 9.0' - the inter-school IT Competition, which was first launched in 2014. This extraordinary IT fest has triumphantly completed seven exceptional years thus far, and now we are thrilled to announce the upcoming ninth edition of Digilogous, scheduled to take place on Saturday, August 05, 2023.
